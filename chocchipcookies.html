Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (if salted, don't add salt)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt (remember, if your butter is salted, don't add this)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 cups flour
  • chocolate chips

Method

  1. Cream butter and sugar together.
  2. Add the eggs to this.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients except the chocolate chips.
  4. Pour chocolate chips in, keeping in mind how chocolatey you want these to be.
  5. Bake at 350 for however long you feel like. (8 minutes should be enough to let them be gooey but not too cooked. It depends on how many chocolate chips you put in.)
