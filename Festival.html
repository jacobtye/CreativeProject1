Jamaican Fried Festival

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp softened butter
  • 1 cup Water
  • Oil for Frying

Method

  1. Mix dry ingredients together
  2. Cut in butter
  3. Add water gradually until it has the consistency of dough
  4. Shape into ovels
  5. Fry in deep frying pan with enough oil to partially cover, turning occasionally until golden
  6. Take out and rest on paper towel to get excess oil
