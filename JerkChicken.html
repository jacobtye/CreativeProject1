Jerk Chicken and Rice and Peas

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 4 Pieces of Chicken
  • Grace Jerk Seasoning Hot
  • Grace Jerk Marinade Mild
  • Dry Jerk Seasoning
  • 1 Whole Jalapeño

Rice and Peas

  • 1 Cans of Coconut Milk
  • 2 Whole Jalapeño
  • 4 Branches of Fresh Thyme
  • 8 Scallion Onions
  • 1/2 cup dried Dark Red Kidney Beans or 2 Cans Dark Red Kidney Beans
  • 2 Cups White Rice

Method

Chicken

  1. Coat Chirck in Dry Jerk Seasoning, Rub it in.
  2. Cover Chicken in Grace Jerk Seasoning
  3. Dice Jalapeño, combine with Chicken in Ziplock bag and marinate for 24hrs
  4. Preheat oven to 325F
  5. Take Chicken out and scrape off hot seasoning
  6. Place Chicken in a baking pan and lightly cover with Jerk Marinade (Lightly don't make it swim)
  7. Cook covered for 45min to 1 hr, or until cooked
    (If needed drain partially part way through to keep liquid down)
  8. Uncover Chicken, drain, and brush on fresh Marinade
  9. Broil for 5 - 10 min or until slightly burnt and marinade caramelizes, coating with more marinade every couple min

Rice and Peas (Kidney Beans)

(If using dried beans)
  1. Place beans in a pot and cover with 4 cups of water and a pinch of salt, soak overnight
  2. Drian Beans, cover with water and low boil till tender
(If using canned beans)
  1. Pour beans into a strainer and rinse
  2. Pour into a pot and cover with water, low boil for 5 - 10 min until tender
(Both)
  1. Save bean liquids
  2. Trim the top and bottoms off the scallion onions
  3. Pock holes in the Jalapeños with a knife or toothpick
  4. Combine coconut milk and the bean liquid to make 4 cups of liquid (add water if necessary)
  5. Mix rice, beans, thyme, onions, jalapeños, and liquid in a large pot
  6. Mix and then bring to a low boil
  7. Boil till rice is cooked
  8. Pick through rice and remove thyme branches and scallion onions
