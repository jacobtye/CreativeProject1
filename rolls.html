Rolls

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2-6 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 Tbsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup softened butter (though you can double the butter if you want)
  • 1 Tbsp yeast
  • 2 cups water

Method

  1. Mix 1/2 cup of the water (make sure it's warm, but not too hot) with the yeast, leave this alone for at least five minutes.
  2. Mix together the salt, sugar, butter, egg, and remaining water.
  3. Add the yeast mixture, mix, and then add three cups of the flour to the mix.
  4. Gradually add flour while mixing or kneading until the dough is no longer sticky to the touch.
  5. Let rise for one hour in a warm, preferably damp or enclosed area. If such an area is not available, saran wrap the bowl to prevent moisture escaping and place in a warm area.
  6. The dough should have almost doubled in this time. If it hasn't, either shape it anyway or let it sit longer and hope it rises. Punch the dough and shape into rolls.
  7. Let the shaped dough rise for an hour.
  8. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees whenever you think prudent, and cook rolls for eight to fourteen minutes.
  9. Either serve hot or let cool and saran wrap or ziploc baggie.
